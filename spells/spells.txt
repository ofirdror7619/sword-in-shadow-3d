🧠 NEXT LEVEL IDEAS (THIS IS WHERE IT GETS REALLY GOOD)
🔗 Spell Synergies (VERY IMPORTANT)
Fire + Wind → bigger explosions
Ice + Lightning → stun + freeze combo
Void + Summon → stronger demons
Curse + Fire → massive burn damage

👉 This makes builds feel unique.

💀 Demon Evolution System (INSANE IDEA FOR YOUR GAME)

Spells change based on corruption level:

Low corruption → normal spells
High corruption → mutated versions

Example:

Firestorm → Hellstorm (bigger, chaotic, harder to control)
Heal → Blood Heal (stronger but costs HP)
