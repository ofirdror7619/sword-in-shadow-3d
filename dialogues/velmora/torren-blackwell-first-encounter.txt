Torren:
You.
You look like you kill things.

Player:
Depends who's asking.

Torren:
Someone who's sick of hoofed filth and hired blades trampling his land.

Player:
Centaurs and warriors?

Torren (spits):
Been a plague for years.
The centaurs keep the forest. The warriors sell it by the body.

Player:
And you want it cleared.

Torren:
I want silence.
No hooves. No arrows. No giant shaking the roots at night.

Player:
What's in it for me?

Torren (grunts):
I pay.
Gold. Relics. Whatever it takes.

(pauses, studies you more closely)

Torren:
But you'll need more than steel for them.

(he reaches into his coat, pulls out a cracked, frost-bitten relic. Faint cold mist leaks from it)

Torren:
They move in packs. Fast. Loud.
Their root-priests hang back and call down light.
Break them.

(he presses the relic into your hand. A pulse of cold spreads up your arm)

Torren (low):
Ice Smash.
Shatters the ground. Slows the herd.

Player:
You're just giving this away?

Torren:
Call it an investment.

(leans in slightly)

Torren:
Bring me proof.
Not stories.

Player:
You hate them that much?

Torren (jaw tightens):
I've buried enough to earn it.

[A tense silence. Wind carries distant animal calls.]

Torren (cold, final):
Go to Hoofgrove.
Clear the centaurs, the warriors, the priests, and the giant behind them.
